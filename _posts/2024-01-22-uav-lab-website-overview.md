## Learning Objectives
A practical website that allows a student to learn and perfect front-end & UI/UX experience. 
- Familiarity with different implementations for front-end development
- Learn how to operate with clientele 
- Perfect and hone UI/UX techniques and make a portfolio worthy piece
- Familiar with development lifecycle via agile/scrum 

## Project Goals
The objective is to create a beautiful website that showcases the UAV Lab and the projects that are currently conducted in the lab. The project is mainly to be used for the lab to provide accurate showcase of what the work to be done for current students to show and find more students interested in the work conducted in the UAV Lab. Marc Cruz will assist in connecting the director of the lab and the team leads to each project. Assisting in communicating all the niche requirements each project may want to showcase. 

## Management
In terms of leadership, the software lead would delegating portions of the work weekly. Having developers syncs weekly at the same time when sprints are coming to an end and gather everyone’s updates. Designed in a way to gain experience in a scrum/sprint structured team.

## Timeline
The timeline will begin when members of the project have been recruited. 
1. Communicating with clientele
	- Getting each projects requirements and deriving a requirement list. 
	- Understanding preferences each project may have.
	- Potentially suggestions on ideas
2. Wireframing and developing rough draft of the website
3. Fleshing out website's illustrations, animations and design
4. Presenting and officializing the website with the UAV Lab Director
