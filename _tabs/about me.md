Experiences derived from the environment. The significance of self-abided discipline to the craft. The pleasure of the succession of one's craft. The undertones of the upbringing of this success: fear, dread, and insignificance. Altering goals into the sensations of dread and bleakness. Hello! My name is Marc Cruz, and I am studying Computer Science at Cal Poly Pomona.

Pursuing an emphasis in Applied Machine Learning / SWE and related fields. This pursuit started with my first interactions with machine learning research in 2022. Since then, I changed majors, oriented my classes towards ML/SWE, joined a professional SWE club, became a student researcher, published a paper, took up various SWE-related work, became a professional club president, became a software team lead for ML object detection, and became a NASA intern for R&D ML applications. The summation of the work I have done excludes my contributions with two startups, establishing five club projects with 50+ members, and being a fellow in Google's FGN Mentorship.

I dedicate my passion from 2023 to the present to my late friend Victor Nguyen and my late grandfather Herminio Cruz. With their funerals being three days apart, this introduced a calamity that tempered stronger ideals in my approaches to life and the work I want to pursue.

Victor Nguyen was a pillar that represented community as he was my mentor and an individual that won't be forgotten. I embody his beliefs, striving to improve the environment I am part of to the best of my abilities, knowing he did the same. Although I can never take on his charisma, I still remember what it meant to show loyalty to those around me and ensure their well-being. Even on the grumpiest days, I always help as quietly as I can. He never needed a thank you and would rather see a friend feel secure and reassured.

Herminio Cruz was the quietest but loudest character in life. Not speaking a word but showing his meticulous mind through the sophisticated designs and variations of Parols. Weeks on end, he would buy more supplies to create a new Parol, all while watching black-and-white Western cowboy movies. The Parol he ended up favoring was a testament to his craftsmanship.

Today, I pursue my career with the same perseverance they both modeled, dedicating myself to the fields of Machine Learning and Software Engineering. I always aim to blend technical rigor with the empathy and loyalty I learned from those who shaped me and from those I work side by side with.
